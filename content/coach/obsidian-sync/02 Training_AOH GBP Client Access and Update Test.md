# AOH GBP Client Access And Update Test

Status: client-zero test
Owner: Local Visibility Manager
Reviewer: Manager
Last updated: 2026-05-21

## What This Is

AOH will test Google Business Profile access on its own profile first before asking clients to do it.

The process should be simple enough for a business owner:

1. Add AOH by email.
2. Do not share Google passwords.
3. Let AOH check the profile and prepare updates.
4. Approve public changes before they go live.

## Agent Ownership

Local Visibility Manager owns:

- Google Business Profile access
- profile health
- profile updates
- posts/photos/services checks
- review links
- local visibility findings
- client handoff proof

Manager owns the brief and blocker tracking.

GHL Expert helps only when GBP needs to connect to HighLevel reputation or workflow pieces.

## Default Access Rule

Default role: **Manager**.

Use Owner only when AOH truly needs admin/user control. That should be a separate approval.

## Client Invite Steps

Client goes to Google Business Profile:

1. Business Profile settings
2. People and access
3. Add
4. Enter AOH Google email
5. Choose Manager
6. Invite

AOH accepts the invite from that Google account.

## AOH Test

Run AOH as client zero:

1. Choose the AOH Google email clients should invite.
2. Add it to AOH's own profile first.
3. Confirm invite and access.
4. Check name, phone, website, hours, categories, services, photos, posts, review link, and unanswered reviews.
5. Draft one safe profile update.
6. Mike approves before posting.
7. Log what was confusing so we can turn it into client instructions.

## Client-Safe Language

> We do not need your Google password. You add our business email as a Manager on your Google Business Profile. That lets us help with profile updates, posts, photos, review links, and visibility checks while you stay in control.
