### Homepage diagnostic — LOCKED 2026-04-29

Sits below the hero, above pricing/offers. The visitor's self-categorization mechanic — they pick their pain, we route them to the matching pillar page.

**Headline:** What is hurting or lacking in your business?

**4 buttons (all-pain framing):**

| # | Button label | Routes to |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Not Enough Bookings | AI Lead Gen offer page |
| 2 | Too Few Reviews | Reviews Starter offer page ($79/mo founding-client) |
| 3 | Low Google Rankings | Heatmap section within AI Lead Gen page |
| 4 | Missed Calls | Voice Agent offer page |

**Why these specifics:**

- **All-pain framing** matches the headline's "hurting or lacking" register. StoryBrand (Donald Miller) + Eugene Schwartz both argue diagnostic UI should mirror the visitor's pain state, not their dream state.
- **Reviews routes to Starter (not Pro)** — $79/mo founding-client price is the low-barrier doorway into GHL/hub360ai. Once a prospect is inside the platform, switching cost compounds. Net Revenue Retention research (Bessemer, ChartMogul) shows land-cheap-expand-inside outperforms premium-first SMB plays.
- **4 short buttons** (~12 words combined) sit comfortably under the 25-word above-fold copy ceiling. Conversion data shows 4 short buttons ≈ 3 buttons; the dropoff is from sentence-length buttons or 5+ buttons.
- **Owner verbatim**: all 4 labels appear in documented owner survey verbatims (AVMA Economic Practice Report, NFDA Member Insights, NADA owner surveys, IBPSA, NFIB Small Business Economic Trends).

**What was rejected and why:**

- "Customers walking in" — only pet groomer has any walk-in component; the other 7 niches are appointment / quote-request / phone-driven.
- "Need help with outreach" — "outreach" is marketer/agency word, not owner verbatim. Owners say "going after new business" or "drumming up customers."
- 5+ buttons — measurable conversion drop above 4 (HubSpot landing-page benchmark: 1 CTA 13.5%, 2-4 CTAs 11.9%, 5+ 10.5%).
- Bundling Reviews + Heatmap into one button — confuses routing; visitor click can't map to one offer.
- Aspiration framing ("More Bookings", "Better Rankings") — mismatch with pain-frame headline.

**Staffing pain — explicit gap (not on homepage):**

Documented #1 pain in 4 of 8 niches (vet, funeral, auto repair, senior living per AVMA / NFDA / NADA / Argentum surveys). Voice Agent offer page must carry the labor-relief framing ("the receptionist you can't hire"). Revisit homepage button list if/when AI Front Desk + Triage becomes a packaged 5th offer.
