## Why This Exists

GetMeFound has moved away from the old AOH "broad AI agency" identity.

GMF should be understood as a local-business visibility and reputation company built for the next version of search: Google, Google Business Profile, reviews, AI answers, and AI-assisted customer actions.

The public offer ladder should be simple:

1. Get Found
2. Stay Found
3. Always Ready

Older labels like Review Power and AI Ready Bundle should stop being public plan names. They can remain internal modules that agents understand, but clients should see the simpler ladder above.

## Positioning

Plain-English positioning:

GetMeFound helps local businesses get found, stay found, and stay ready as Google and AI change how customers choose who to call.

Internal positioning:

GMF sells visibility upkeep, reputation proof, and AI-readiness for local businesses. It does not sell itself as a generic AI agency. It does not sell cold outreach or social prospecting as a client service under GMF; those are internal growth systems unless Mike approves a separate brand.

## Customer Promise

GMF does not promise rankings, revenue, or a specific number of reviews.

GMF does promise:

- clearer Google-facing business facts
- a cleaner Google Business Profile
- a safer review request path
- consistent upkeep so the profile does not go stale
- owner-friendly proof of what changed
- approval-gated AI/reply/voice behavior

## Public Offer Ladder

| Offer | Price | Best For | Client Outcome |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Get Found | $149 one-time | Business needs the basics fixed first | Google profile, business facts, review path, and AI visibility baseline are cleaned up |

## Offer 1: Get Found

### Purpose

Get Found is the low-risk first step. It fixes the visible business basics before GMF asks the client to commit to monthly service.

### Price

$149 one-time.

Recommended timing language:

- work begins within 48 hours after purchase
- typical completion is 48-72 hours after access and intake are ready
- no contract and no recurring charge

### Client Gets

- Google Business Profile audit and cleanup plan
- business name, address/service area, phone, website, category, hours, and services reviewed
- profile description, services, photos/logo, and obvious trust signals checked
- website-to-Google consistency check
- LocalBusiness/schema or developer handoff recommendation where relevant
- Google review link captured
- first email review request setup or one approved starter batch if the client provides a usable list
- AI/search visibility baseline snapshot
- before/after summary
- 30-minute onboarding call

### Not Included

- ongoing monthly profile monitoring
- weekly Google posts
- ongoing review campaigns
- SMS/text review requests
- review reply drafting after the first setup
- paid ads
- full website rebuild
- guaranteed Google ranking or review count

### Agent Ownership

Primary agent: Profile Manager

Supporting agents:

- Manager routes the job and watches blockers
- Auditor checks proof before Done
- Client Success can turn proof into a client-safe recap

### Done Means

- client access/intake status is recorded
- Google Business Profile facts were checked
- review link is captured or blocker is recorded
- website/profile consistency check is complete
- before/after proof exists
- client receives the summary

## Offer 2: Stay Found

### Purpose

Stay Found keeps the business from going stale after the first cleanup. It is the core monthly plan and should be the default recommendation for most clients.

### Price

$99/month plus $49 one-time setup fee.

Month-to-month. Cancel anytime.

For clients who start directly on Stay Found, the Get Found-style baseline setup is included during launch.

### Client Gets

- everything in Get Found included during launch
- weekly client list upload path for review requests
- email review request campaigns
- text/SMS review request campaigns after phone-number, compliance, opt-out, and approval requirements are ready
- text-message setup handled by GMF when approved
- AI review response drafts in the client's brand voice
- no auto-posted review replies unless Mike and the client explicitly approve that later
- negative review alert and suggested response target within 4 business hours
- one Google Business Profile post per week
- review monitoring across platforms where available
- monthly visibility and reputation report
- client hub/status page where available
- basic profile drift check

### Important SMS Rule

The current public site says Stay Found includes text and email review requests.

Recommended operating interpretation:

SMS is included as a Stay Found capability, but SMS does not go live until phone-number approval, opt-in language, STOP handling, sample approval, and compliance checks are complete. If SMS is not ready, the email review path can still run.

This keeps the public offer simple without letting agents send risky texts too early.

### Not Included

- live AI voice agent
- broad content strategy beyond the weekly Google post
- custom CRM/POS integration beyond the approved manual or standard upload path
- paid ads
- legal/reputation crisis management
- guaranteed review count

### Agent Ownership

Primary agents:

- Profile Manager owns Google profile upkeep
- Reviews Manager owns review request readiness and proof
- Reply Writer drafts review replies
- Client Success owns monthly recap/report

Supporting agents:

- Manager routes work and escalates only exceptions
- Systems Director checks sender, storage, compliance, and automation health
- Auditor verifies proof and safety gates

### Done Means Each Month

- review request path checked
- send proof or blocker recorded
- weekly Google post proof exists or blocker recorded
- review monitoring checked
- reply drafts created when needed
- negative review escalation handled when needed
- monthly report delivered or ready for approval

## Offer 3: Always Ready

### Purpose

Always Ready is the full-service readiness plan. It prepares the business for AI search, AI answers, Google-assisted calling, and customer questions that expect clear business facts.

### Price

$299/month.

Month-to-month. Cancel anytime. No setup fee unless Mike later decides otherwise.

### Client Gets

- everything in Stay Found
- AI voice agent/readiness build trained on services, pricing, hours, FAQs, and escalation rules
- voice and phone readiness for AI-assisted and customer inquiries
- full Google Business Profile content management
- local content planning
- FAQ updates and location-page recommendations
- monthly 30-minute strategy call
- monthly AI answer visibility check
- monthly readiness notes showing what facts, content, or profile signals should be improved next

### AI Voice Safety Rule

Always Ready includes voice readiness and buildout. It does not mean live AI voice automation is enabled by default.

Live AI voice behavior stays approval-gated per client. Nothing should answer calls, speak to leads, or represent the client live until the client and Mike approve the live behavior, escalation rules, fallback path, and risk limits.

### Not Included

- automatic live AI calling without explicit approval
- custom software buildouts beyond GMF's standard readiness stack
- paid ads
- full SEO agency deliverables outside local visibility/readiness
- legal, medical, financial, or regulated advice
- guaranteed AI answer placement

### Agent Ownership

Primary agents:

- Manager owns the plan and escalation gates
- Profile Manager owns profile/content readiness
- Reviews Manager owns reputation/review systems
- Reply Writer owns approval-first review replies
- Systems Director owns voice/tool/safety readiness
- Coach keeps the client facts, FAQs, and knowledge layer clean
- Client Success owns strategy-call notes and monthly recap

Supporting agent:

- Auditor verifies proof and blocks risky live behavior

### Done Means Each Month

- Stay Found work is complete or blockers are recorded
- AI answer visibility check is complete
- content/readiness recommendations are recorded
- voice readiness status is current
- strategy-call notes or owner recap are delivered
- any live AI/voice action has explicit approval proof

## Client Journey

### 1. Prospecting

Goal: find potential clients.

Examples:

- Smartlead readiness
- cold email campaigns
- lead list building
- reply sorting
- booked-call handoff

This is internal GMF growth work, not a client deliverable.

### 2. Sales And Signup

Goal: convert prospect to paying client.

Examples:

- discovery call prep
- plan recommendation
- checkout/payment verification
- intake link
- client ID and folder creation after payment

### 3. Client Onboarding

Goal: get access safely.

Examples:

- Google Business Profile manager access
- website access if needed
- review link capture
- customer upload/list path
- client facts/FAQs
- compliance blockers

### 4. Launch / First 14 Days

Goal: deliver first visible value.

Examples:

- Get Found cleanup
- first review request setup
- first Google post
- client hub/proof page

### 5. Recurring Runs

Goal: keep the account moving.

Examples:

- weekly Google post
- review request readiness
- profile drift check
- review monitoring
- monthly report
- monthly AI visibility check for Always Ready

### 6. Client Success / Reports

Goal: make value obvious and retain the account.

Examples:

- monthly recap
- review/sentiment summary
- visibility report
- at-risk notes

Goal: recommend the next level only when it truly helps.

Examples:

- voice readiness packet
- AI visibility/content gap recommendation

## Human Approval Rules

Manager should contact Mike only when a human decision is actually needed.

Mike approval is required for:

- new spend or tool subscription
- credentials/access that only Mike can provide
- client-facing promises that are unclear
- risky public profile edits
- first live review request campaign for a client
- any live SMS/text campaign before compliance is proven
- review replies being posted publicly
- live AI voice or auto-reply behavior
- billing/refund/contract exceptions
- legal, safety, medical, financial, or reputation-crisis topics
- enabling any HighLevel AI feature

Everything else should be handled by Manager and specialist agents with proof recorded.

## Proof Rules

No job is Done without proof.

Minimum proof by plan:

- Get Found: before/after snapshot, access/intake status, profile check, review link or blocker
- Stay Found: send proof or blocker, post proof, review monitoring check, monthly report
- Always Ready: Stay Found proof plus AI visibility check, readiness notes, voice/content proof, approval proof for any live AI behavior

## Recommended Owner Decisions

These are the few points Mike should approve or adjust before this becomes the official source of truth:

1. Keep the public ladder as Get Found, Stay Found, Always Ready.
2. Retire Review Power and AI Ready Bundle as public plan names.
3. Keep SMS/text inside Stay Found, but gate all SMS behind compliance and approval.
4. Use "work begins within 48 hours" and "typical completion 48-72 hours after access/intake" for Get Found.
5. Treat website hosting as included only when GMF is managing a GMF-hosted page/site, not as a blanket hosting promise for every outside website.
6. Treat Always Ready voice as readiness/buildout by default, with live voice automation requiring explicit approval.
