# Presence Refresh Product Runbook

Status: build now
Owner: Press
Reviewer: Coach + Systems Director
Last updated: 2026-05-21

## Plain-English Offer

Presence Refresh is a one-time setup product:

> Before we drive new attention to your business, we make sure you do not look inactive when prospects check you out.

This is not the main lead engine. It supports Reach by making the client look active, credible, and current before outreach sends people to their website or social pages.

## Current Launch Special

AOH should be client zero first. Refresh AOH, save before/after proof, then use the same process with founding clients.

Normal starter package:

- ~~5 social posts~~
- ~~1 useful blog post~~

Founding client launch special:

- 10 social posts
- 5 useful blog posts
- Morning Brief bonus showing what changed, proof links, open approvals, and the next Reach move
- Limited-time founding price: $499 one time, with $997 crossed out as the standard package value

After proof is strong, move this toward $1,500-$2,500 depending on channels, blog depth, publishing access, and whether AOH connects the work into Reach or GHL.

## Why It Fits AOH

- It is easy for SMB owners to understand.
- It creates visible before/after proof.
- It lowers friction before Cold Email Reach or Social Reach.
- It is safer and easier to deliver than automated social engagement.
- It can be sold as setup, not another monthly dashboard.

## Best Fit Clients

- Dead or stale social pages.
- No recent website/blog content.
- Good business, weak online proof.
- Client about to start Cold Email Reach or Social Reach.

## What The Client Gets

Launch-special package:

1. Short online presence audit.
2. Ten catch-up social posts.
3. Five useful blog posts.
4. Light brand cleanup recommendations.
5. Proof links/screenshots after approval and publish.
6. Morning Brief proof summary.
7. Recommended next Reach angle.

## Agent Flow

| Step | Owner | What happens | Proof |
|---|---|---|---|
| Audit the business presence | Scout | Check website, social pages, last post dates, service pages, obvious trust gaps, and competitors. | Audit notes and screenshots. |
| Pick the message angle | Coach | Choose one useful customer-facing theme. | Approved theme and audience note. |
| Create the fill pack | Editor | Draft 10 social posts and 5 blog posts. | Draft pack ready for approval. |
| Publish after approval | Press | Publish or schedule only after approval. | Published links and screenshots. |
| Hand off to Reach | Manager | Use the refreshed content as credibility for outreach. | Campaign-ready summary. |

## Guardrails

- Do not publish without approval.
- Do not claim results, awards, licenses, or guarantees the client did not provide.
- Use client-owned channels only after access is confirmed.
- Keep the blog helpful and specific, not keyword-stuffed.
- Keep Google Business Profile and review work separate unless the client bought that package.

## Pricing Direction

|---|---:|
