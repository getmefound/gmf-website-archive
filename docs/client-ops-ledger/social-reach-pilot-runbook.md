# Social Reach Pilot Runbook

Status: pilot
Owner: Scout
Reviewer: Sales Manager + Systems Director
Last updated: 2026-05-21

## Plain-English Offer

Social Reach is a recurring Reach-style product:

> We find the conversations your future customers are already having and help your business show up usefully before competitors do.

This is not automated spam. The v1 product is community listening, opportunity scoring, helpful comment drafting, and human approval.

## Why It Fits AOH

- It is warmer than cold outreach.
- It builds trust before pitching.
- It creates customer-language insights for Cold Email Reach, blogs, ads, and offers.
- It can become a premium monthly add-on if it creates conversations.

## Best Fit Clients

- B2B or local service businesses where trust matters.
- Owners with useful expertise but no time to watch social conversations.
- Companies already selling through relationships, referrals, or education.
- Clients who can approve comments quickly.

## What The Client Gets

1. Approved source watchlist.
2. Conversation opportunity queue.
3. Helpful comment drafts.
4. Light social selling playbook.
5. Weekly opportunity and outcome report.

## Agent Flow

| Step | Owner | What happens | Proof |
|---|---|---|---|
| Build the watchlist | Scout | Identify approved groups, pages, posts, hashtags, newsletters, and feeds. | Watchlist with source, platform, access owner, and posting rules. |
| Find useful conversations | Scout | Surface questions where the business can help. | Opportunity queue with links and reason to engage. |
| Score the opportunity | Sales Manager | Keep only real buyer pain, local fit, or authority value. | Score for help value, fit, urgency, and promotion risk. |
| Draft a helpful response | Coach | Draft a response that answers first and lightly offers help only when natural. | Draft response plus why it is safe. |
| Human posts or approves | Client or AOH owner | A real human posts from the real account. | Approved/post link saved. |
| Track trust signals | Manager | Track replies, DMs, booked calls, and useful buyer language. | Weekly Social Reach summary. |

## Hard Guardrails

- No auto-commenting.
- No auto-liking.
- No fake accounts.
- No mass scraping.
- No posting in groups that ban business participation.
- Human approval before posting during v1.
- Answer the question first. Pitch only when it naturally fits.

LinkedIn is explicit that bots, scraping, fake accounts, and automated posting/commenting can violate its rules. Meta/Facebook also restricts automated data collection. Treat these as hard product boundaries, not suggestions.

## Pilot Criteria

Start with AOH before selling:

1. Build 10 approved sources.
2. Find 25 conversation opportunities.
3. Draft 10 helpful comments.
4. Human-post only the best 3-5.
5. Track replies, DMs, profile views, useful language, and booked calls.
6. Decide if this is worth monthly packaging.

## Pricing Direction

Pilot manually first.

If it works, suggested pricing:

| Package | Price |
|---|---:|
| Light watch | $500-$750/month |
| Active Social Reach | $950-$1,500/month |
| Custom social selling team | $1,500+/month |

Price depends on sources watched, comment volume, approval speed, and whether AOH posts or only drafts.
