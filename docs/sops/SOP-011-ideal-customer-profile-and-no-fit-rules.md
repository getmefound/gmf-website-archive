## Purpose

Keep GMF focused on prospects that fit the offer, can be helped safely, and are worth onboarding.

## Trigger

New prospect source, niche, campaign idea, partner audience, inbound lead, or manual referral.

## Fit Signals

- Local business where Google Business Profile, reviews, website facts, and visibility proof matter.
- Owner/operator can act on access requests and customer-list requests.
- Business has a real public footprint or needs a foundation cleanup.
- Get Found, Stay Found, or Always Ready clearly solves a present visibility/reputation problem.
- No heavy regulated, legal, medical, financial, adult, political, crisis, or high-liability claims needed.

## No-Fit Or Hold Signals

- Wants guaranteed rankings, guaranteed reviews, or guaranteed revenue.
- Needs full SEO agency scope, ads management, web rebuild, or complex ecommerce build as the first ask.
- Has unresolved legal/reputation crisis beyond GMF's scope.
- Is a direct competitor, self-referral abuser, spam source, or unclear identity.
- Cannot provide access, ownership proof, or basic business facts.
- Requires HighLevel AI auto-replies or paid AI features without Mike approval.

## Procedure

1. Identify business type, location, offer need, source, website, GBP, and owner/contact.
2. Mark as `fit`, `possible fit`, `hold`, or `no-fit`.
3. If possible fit, request only the minimum extra context needed.
4. If no-fit, stop outreach or decline kindly.
5. If risky but valuable, route to Sales Manager/Manager before any promise.

## Required Proof

- Fit/no-fit decision
- Reason
- Source
- Owner
- Next allowed action
