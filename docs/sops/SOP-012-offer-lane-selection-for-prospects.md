## Purpose

Route prospects into the right GMF offer lane without overselling or under-scoping.

## Trigger

Prospect enters pipeline or asks what they should buy.

## Offer Lanes

| Lane | Use When |
|---|---|
| Get Found | Foundation is messy: GBP, facts, review link, visibility basics, AI/Search readiness baseline |
| Stay Found | Foundation exists and they need recurring reviews, GBP posts, monitoring, and monthly proof |
| Always Ready | They need deeper AI/local visibility, replies, SMS/A2P, strategy, or lead-response readiness |
| Nurture | Fit exists but timing/budget/urgency is not ready |
| No-fit | Scope, risk, or expectations do not match GMF |

## Procedure

1. Review source, report, business facts, pain, and budget/timing signal.
2. Select one primary lane.
3. Document why the lane fits.
4. If recommending upgrade or higher plan, include evidence, not pressure.
5. Route custom scope to Sales Manager before any promise.

## Required Proof

- Selected lane
- Reason
- Supporting evidence
- Next action

## Communication Rule

Use plain wording: "Based on what we can see, the useful first step is..." Avoid claiming Google, AI systems, or customers will behave a certain way.
