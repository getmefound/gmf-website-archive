## Short Message

```text
Hey [Name], I’m building out GetMeFound’s client workflow and would like to use your business as a friendly test case.

We would treat it carefully like a real client, but label it internally as a non-billing test pilot. We would not need your Google password. If Google Business Profile access is needed, you would add our business email as a Manager so you stay in control.

Before we make any public changes, we’ll show you what we recommend and get your approval.

If you’re good with that, can you send me:

- business name
- website
- best contact email
- phone number you want public
- main service/category
- city or service area
- current Google Business Profile link, if you have it
- top 3 services
- anything you do NOT want changed publicly

No pressure if you’d rather not. I’m using this to test the workflow safely before we put more clients through it.
```

## Follow-Up If They Say Yes

```text
Great. First step is just a baseline visibility check. We’ll look at what is already public: your website, Google profile, business facts, reviews, and obvious visibility gaps.

If we need Google Business Profile access later, I’ll send you simple instructions. You will not share your password.
```

## Boundary Reminder

Do not send review requests, make profile edits, or ask for customer lists until the friend explicitly agrees to that part of the pilot.
