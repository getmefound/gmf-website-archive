## Purpose

Route prospects who ask to talk without creating calendar chaos or bypassing fit/scope checks.

## Trigger

Prospect asks for a call, demo, consultation, or "can we talk?"

## Procedure

1. Confirm prospect identity, business, source, and fit status.
2. Check whether a free report or pricing answer would solve the question faster.
3. If call is useful, mark call requested and route to Manager/Mike path.
4. Send a short acknowledgment and ask for any missing context.
5. Log requested call, topic, urgency, and owner.

## Required Proof

- Reply/request
- Fit status
- Call reason
- Assigned owner
- Response sent

## Failure Or Blocker Handling

- No-fit prospect: decline or nurture; do not book by default.
- Custom scope: Sales Manager reviews first.
- Client/support issue: route to Account Manager, not Sales Rep.
